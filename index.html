Role Clarity

CEE (Orchestrator) CESA (Accelerator)
  • Owns value realization strategy.
  • Identifies risk and expansion signals.
  • Translates technical capabilities into outcomes.
  • Diagnoses adoption/workflow gaps.

The 4-Step Engagement Model

1. Define

CEE identifies objectives and renewal risk.

2. Align

Define roles, messaging, and expected deliverable.

3. Interact

CEE frames business; CESA leads technical translation.

4. Drive

CESA delivers; CEE updates Success Plan within 48h.
