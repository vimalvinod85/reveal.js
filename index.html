When to Route to Support

  • Broken items, defects, or outages.
  • Unexpected system behavior.
  • Requires a technical support ticket.

When to Route to CESA (SA)

  • Outcome mapping and workflow alignment.
  • Value articulation and executive narratives.
  • Solution approach trade-offs.
