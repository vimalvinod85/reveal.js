The 4-Step Engagement Model

1. Define

CEE identifies objectives, risk level, and timing.

2. Align

Define roles, messaging, and expected deliverable.

3. Interact

CEE frames business; CESA leads technical mapping.

4. Drive

Deliver agreed assets; CEE updates the Success Plan.
